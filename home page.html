Welcome to our Seven Colour Food Market Sundays event page

Seven Colour Food Market Sundays is the ideal destination for an amazing Sunday out with friends and family. Held at Greenside Park, this Food and Lifestyle Market offers quality food, beverages, good music, and wonderful people. Come have the finest time in town dancing to excellent music and eating delectable meals.

Gates open at 12:00pm. Tickets cost R50 online and R100 at the gate.
Cooler boxes are allowed at the venue. Tickets available on Computicket.
